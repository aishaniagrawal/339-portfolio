About Me

Hi! My name is Aishani Agrawal and I'm currently a junior studying UX Design at the University of Michigan. For as long as I can remember, my family has loved traveling and exploring new places in the world. My parents instilled a love of traveling in me from a young age, when we would take frequent trips to India to visit family.


Throughout the years, we have gone on trips around Europe, Asia, and other countries in North America. I would love to eventually visit all 7 continents and hope to be able to see all 7 wonders of the world. I wanted to make this guide to find more unique, picturesque places both in the United States and outside of it.


From January to June of 2023 I'm going to be studying abroad in Sweden and I could not be more excited! Although I've been to a few countries in Europe, I want to use this as an opportunity to explore other countries in Scandinavia and more unique places that I have not visited.
